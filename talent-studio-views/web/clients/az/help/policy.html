Data protection laws govern the processing of personal information within Europe and other parts of the world. ("Personal information" means information that relates to living individuals and "processing" is widely defined to include the use, collection and disclosure of personal information.) Failure to comply with data protection laws could result in enforcement action being taken against AstraZeneca and, in certain circumstances, against you personally. Further, breach of the Code of Conduct and AstraZeneca's Global Policy on Data Protection and Privacy is a disciplinary offence. For these reasons you must comply with the following requirements:

a. Purpose - The information on the system may only be used to support the Global Commercial and AstraZeneca Talent Review processes, including, management planning, the identification of candidates for leadership vacancies, discussions about the quality of the top talent pool and high potential individuals.

b. Accuracy and relevance - Any information that you record in the system about an individual, especially in any free text field, must be factually accurate, relevant and kept up-to-date. You should only comment on the performance of an individual in an objective way. Please remember that an individual has the right to request a copy of any personal information which we process about him or her, including the information held in this system.

c. Disclosure and access - The data on the system should only be made available to those individuals who have a legitimate business need to be granted such access. The data on the system should only be disclosed to the extent necessary for the purposes described in paragraph (a).

d. Further information - if you have any questions, or require further clarification about the extent and nature of your obligations please speak to your Compliance Officer.
