Welcome Note


The Talent Management system provides a global process that ensures we are assessing and identifying top talent across 3B.

Our shared approach to Performance and Talent Management is embedded in our annual business cycle.

Within 3B this enables us to:
  • Differentiate people based on their performance and their potential tracked over time
  • Identify talent earlier in careers enabling us to develop a diverse talent pool
  • Obtain a common benchmark across 3B to identify talent leading to more opportunities
  • Provide tailored and actionable development plans to meet 3B criteria for optimal performance
The fundamental aspect is about having conversations with individuals, discussing their performance and formulating their development plans. This system enhances this process by giving you access to the information that is the key to good quality performance and talent management.
