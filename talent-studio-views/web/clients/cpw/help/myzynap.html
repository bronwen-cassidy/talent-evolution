Talent Management




Talent Management has been identified as one of the CPW three strategic people priorities.
In order for CPW to continue to grow, it is recognised that we need to take a more managed
approach to the way we identify, develop and retain key talent.



This on-line system will enhance our current processes by supporting management planning
decisions, ensuring we have a calibrated list of high potential individuals and provide us with
a mechanism for sharing information between businesses and functions about key individuals
and their aspirations.



If you have any questions about the system, please contact your local HR team.
