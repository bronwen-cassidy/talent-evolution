Oracle EMEA is committed to Talent Management.

Over then past few years we have been driving a Talent Management philosophy across the organisation -  this is evident today at a regional, functional and country level with Talent Review Boards being held, allowing senior leadership teams, visibility of, and intelligence about, people and business critical roles that we should be investing in.

It is imperative that as a organisation we have a specific understanding of the current and planned state of the business - so we are better able to develop our internal talent pool and safeguard key roles which are both vital for driving our business forward.

Through this process we are able to:

  • identify and develop our leadership and management capabilities
  • improve the retention of key contributors via targeted development and career opportunities
  • improve organisational performance through the improved performance of individuals
  • increase our attractiveness as a employer of choice - both internally and externally.

Talent Management is fast becoming a global concern for Oracle.
