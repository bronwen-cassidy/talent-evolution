Mission Statement

The Talent Management system provides a global process to ensure that we are assessing and identifying top talent across the corporation. Performance and Talent Management continues to be a priority for our business as we move from transformation to growth.

Please ensure that you have read the user handbook and the data protection user guide before using the Talent Management System.

The information on this system is used to support the Talent Review process where management planning, the identification of candidates for leadership vacancies and the quality of the top talent pool and high potentials is discussed. Any information recorded on this system must be accurate and relevant.
