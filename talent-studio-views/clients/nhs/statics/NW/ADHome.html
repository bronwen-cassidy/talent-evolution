The NHS North West Leadership Academy

The purpose of the Academy is to create a platform for excellence in leadership and strategy development in the North West. The Academy is for the NHS and owned by the NHS, it will encourage innovation and seek out excellence in leadership thinking. The Academy will achieve world-class health leadership through systematic development of people with highest potential to progress.
We will showcase expertise in the North West to raise the profile of the leading edge practices. The Academy will promote strategic alliances in leadership, for example; North West public sector, business community, academia and the independent sector e will provide peer group support for NHS leads. The Academy is committed to improve the diversity of the leadership population in the NW. We will ensure strong clinical leaders and encourage clinical leadership at the most senior levels by promoting clinician and management alliances

Talent Studio

Talent Studio provides a comprehensive talent management solution that will be used to capture, integrate, present, and assess employment, performance, succession, and career planning information. The hosted web application brings together employee information from a wide range of sources, and makes it available for executive-level talent review, assessment, and assignment processes at all levels. Talent Studio provides a best practice platform to use in talent management processes.
The advantages of the Talent Studio is that the information is made available to:
  • Staff who can easily enter and review pertinent data such as their qualifications, experience and aspirations.
  • Line and staff managers who can easily enter and review employee performance, suitability for future roles and development data.
  • Senior managers who can easily retrieve and review organizational data on all staff including leadership potential, key skill holders and critical competencies.
  • HR managers who can easily define, configure, and administer the "pool" of employee talent in the organisation.
The benefits of Talent Studio include: improved use of existing resources, employee development and assignment, employee retention, and performance.

Terms of Use

Talent Studio is the software of choice for the Academy and provides one mechanism for identifying and tracking the North West talent pool. Those individuals who do not have access to Talent Studio should not be precluded from applying for senior leadership posts.
