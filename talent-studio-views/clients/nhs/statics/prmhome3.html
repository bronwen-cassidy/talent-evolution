Graduate Management Training Scheme

Welcome to Talent Studio - the Trainee Performance Portfolio

Talent Studio forms the trainee's performance portfolio that develops as they progress through the scheme, capturing information such as performance reviews, progress in their specialism competencies, placement planning, placement job descriptions and objectives, personal development plans, academic achievements and feedback from the Assessment Centre.

The benefits of Talent Studio include:
  • Programme Managers are able to remotely monitor their trainee's progress and performance for the duration of the scheme even when the trainee is placed in another organisation
  • Placement Managers are able to access a range of information about their trainees from previous activities and placements and are able to use this to form the basis of their performance reviews and inform their personal development plans
  • Most SHAs have now chosen Talent Studio to support their Leadership Programmes allowing the potential for trainees to be an integral part of the talent pipeline as they progress through the scheme and on to further careers within the NHS

Talent Studio and Scheme Activities

Email bulletins will be sent periodically from your scheme area office to remind you of upcoming scheme activities relevant to you and your trainees.

Talent Studio Training and User Guide

Your Leadership Development Manager should arrange training with you; this may be delivered as part of a briefing or at a network meeting, or alternatively it can be delivered via an on-line conferencing facility if you are unable to make a face to face meeting.

Please contact your scheme area office to find out what options are available to you.

The Talent Studio User Guide should be used in conjunction with the Scheme Handbook which is located on the Graduate Scheme Website.

Both the Handbook and the User Guide have been significantly updated so please take time to read them.
